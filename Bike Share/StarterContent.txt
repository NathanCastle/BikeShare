====FAQ====
<div class="panel-group" id="accordion">
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading">
            <h4 class="panel-title">
                <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapse1">Is this a free service?</a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse in" id="collapse1">
            <div class="panel-body">
                <p>Yes and no.</p>
                <p>Yes in that it is free to use the bike share.</p>
                <p>No in that there are charges for major damage to the bike or failure to return it in a timely fashion.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading">
            <h4 class="panel-title">
                <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapse2"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-user"></span> Who can join this program?</a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapse2">
            <div class="panel-body">
                <p>Any RIT Student, Faculty, or Staff can borrow a bike!</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading">
            <h4 class="panel-title">
                <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapse3"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-usd"></span> Are there any fees associated with renting a bicycle?</a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapse3">
            <div class="panel-body">
                <p>The Rental Service is free; however, there are possible charges for misuse of bikes or the service, These are are outlined in the rental agreement and copied below.</p>

                <div class="form-horizontal">
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Late return:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">$5 per 24 hours, not including the holidays/weekends.</div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Failure to return bike after 4 weeks:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">The cost of replacing the bike (Approx. $350).</div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Lost Key:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">$30, to replace the U-Lock.</div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Damaged Key:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">$5, to replace the key.</div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Lost Bike:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">The cost of replacing the bike (Approx. $350).</div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="form-group">
                        <div class="col-xs-4"><label class="control-label">Irreperable Bike Damage:</label></div>
                        <div class="col-xs-8">The cost of replacing the bike (Approx. $350).</div>
                    </div>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading">
            <h4 class="panel-title">
                <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapse4"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-headphones"></span> Do I need to wear a helmet?</a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapse4">
            <div class="panel-body">
                <p>Wearing a helmet is strongly recommended. Helmets are not included in the rental.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading">
            <h4 class="panel-title">

        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapse5">

                <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapse6"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-question-sign"></span> Is there anything that I need to do before borrowing a bike?</a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapse6">
            <div class="panel-body">
